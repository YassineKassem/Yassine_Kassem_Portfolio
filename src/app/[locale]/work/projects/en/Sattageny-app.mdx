## Overview

Sattageny is Tunisia's first mobile application dedicated to intelligent internship placement. Designed during the COVID-19 pandemic to address inefficiencies in internship hunting and recruitment, Sattageny integrates advanced recommendation algorithms to match students with internship opportunities that align with their skills and aspirations.

Developed as part of a professional final-year project with **Medianet**, Sattageny caters specifically to the IT domain. It automates the tedious processes of CV management and candidate matching for companies, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.

## Key Features

- **Student-Centric Design**: 
  - Allows students to create or upload CVs.
  - Enables internship searches based on domain, location, and skills.
  - Recommends internships based on CV analysis using AI.
- **Company Integration**: 
  - Simplifies posting and managing internship offers.
  - Automated candidate matching based on offer requirements.
- **Smart Recommendation System**: 
  - Analyzes CVs and job descriptions to recommend the best-fit opportunities and candidates.
  - Generates similarity scores for transparency.
- **Administrative Oversight**: 
  - Comprehensive dashboard for managing user accounts and applications.

## Technologies Used

- **Mobile Development**: Flutter for cross-platform application development.
- **Backend Services**: Node.js and Flask for robust APIs.
- **Database**: MongoDB for flexible and scalable data storage.
- **Recommendation Algorithms**: Python-based vectorization and data cleaning pipelines.
- **UI/UX**: Designed for simplicity and seamless navigation.

## Methodology

Sattageny was built following the **Scrum methodology** to ensure iterative development and client collaboration. The development process was divided into five sprints:
1. **CV and Company Creation**: Student and company account setup.
2. **Internship Management**: Creating, editing, and managing offers.
3. **AI-Driven Recommendations**: Development of recommendation engines.
4. **Application Management**: Managing and tracking applications.
5. **Profile Management**: Comprehensive user and company profile handling.

## Challenges and Innovations

- **Data Quality**: Overcoming challenges in parsing diverse CV formats with text extraction and cleaning tools like Apache Tika.
- **Recommendation Accuracy**: Implemented advanced natural language processing to ensure relevance and precision.
- **Scalability**: Built an architecture robust enough to handle increasing user demands.

## Outcome

Sattageny successfully bridges the gap between students and companies in Tunisia. It has been praised for:
- Reducing the time for internship placement by 70%.
- Enhancing the quality of candidate selection for companies.
- Offering students a more transparent and responsive application process.

By integrating AI, user-friendly interfaces, and practical features, Sattageny sets a benchmark for internship platforms in Tunisia.
